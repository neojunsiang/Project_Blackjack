WELCOME TO BLACKJACK

Your Objective - Get a hand of higher points without exceeding 21 points!


Point System
2 - 10: Number on card represent the point
J, Q, K: Represent as 10 points
Ace: 11 points for 2 cards on hand, 10 points for 3 cards on hand and 1 point for 4 cards and above


Keys
Start - Start Game
Hit - Hit card
Continue - Continue game after round ends
End - Restart game
